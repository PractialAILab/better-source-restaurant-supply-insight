Common Questions

What counts as "local sourcing"?

We define local sourcing as ingredients from farms, ranches, dairies, and producers within your state or regional area. We verify your sourcing relationships during the approval process.

How do you calculate the percentage for the colored badges?

We calculate based on the number of menu categories you source locally (beef, pork, chicken, seafood, breads, produce, dairy, desserts, appetizers). Green badges = 75%+ local, Orange = 50-74%, Red = under 50%.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable but you keep your listing for the full year.

How do Premium coupons work?

Each month, premium members upload a promotional coupon image (e.g., "10% off dinner", "Free appetizer"). We display it when customers click your restaurant on the map. You can update it monthly to keep offers fresh.

What if my local sourcing changes?

Simply contact us and we'll update your listing. We want your information to always be accurate and current.
